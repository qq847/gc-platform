💬 Frequently Asked Questions
How do I add attendees manually? ▼
Go to Attendees panel → click + Invite to send an invitation, or use ⬆ Import CSV to bulk import from a spreadsheet.
How do I create a paid ticket? ▼
Enable the Paid Ticket module in Event Editor, then go to the Paid Ticket panel to set prices, capacity, and discount codes.
Can I duplicate an event? ▼
Yes! In My Events, hover over any event card and click the 📋 duplicate icon. A copy will be created with "Copy of" prefix.
How does the Buyer Invitation module work? ▼
The Buyer Invitation module lets you invite qualified buyers (publishers, investors) to your event. They are matched with exhibitors via the AI-powered Let's Meet module for 1:1 meetings.
How do I export data? ▼
Each panel has an Export CSV button in the top-right area. The Attendees panel also supports bulk export of selected attendees.
