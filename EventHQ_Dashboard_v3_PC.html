❓ Frequently Asked Questions
GDC 2027 is designed for game developers, publishers, investors, platform holders, and service providers across the global games industry. Whether you’re an indie studio or a AAA publisher, there’s something for you.
All tickets include access to the exhibition floor, conference sessions, and networking events. VIP All-Access tickets additionally include the Awards Gala dinner, VIP lounge access, and priority meeting scheduling.
Our AI-powered Let’s Meet system matches you with the most relevant attendees based on your profile, interests, and goals. You can request meetings in advance, and confirmed meetings are automatically scheduled into your personal agenda.
Booth and sponsorship applications can be submitted directly through this page. Free booths are allocated on a first-come, first-served basis. Paid booth and sponsorship packages include additional benefits such as logo placement, speaking slots, and dedicated meeting pods.
Full refunds are available up to 60 days before the event. 50% refunds are available 30–60 days before. No refunds within 30 days of the event, but tickets can be transferred to another person at no charge.

Join 3,500+ game industry professionals. Early Bird tickets available until March 31.
