Frequently Asked Questions

How does the purchase process work? ▼
After submitting your interest form, our sponsorship manager will contact you within 1 business day to confirm details and send a formal invoice. No upfront payment required to reserve.
Can I customize a package? ▼
Absolutely! Contact Qiang Qiang directly to discuss custom add-ons, combine tiers, or build a bespoke sponsorship that fits your specific goals and budget.
What are the payment terms? ▼
We accept bank transfer and card payment. A 50% deposit secures your slot, with the balance due 30 days before the event. Full payment options available on request.
What if the event is cancelled? ▼
In the unlikely event of cancellation, all sponsorship fees are fully refunded or can be applied to the next edition of Game Connection.
When will I receive branding assets guidelines? ▼
Upon signing, you'll receive a Sponsor Onboarding Kit with all asset deadlines, file specifications, and a dedicated contact for logistics and production.
📋 What happens next?
  1. Submit your interest form
  2. Receive formal sponsorship agreement
  3. Sign & pay 50% deposit
  4. Get your Sponsor Onboarding Kit
  5. Go live at GC Europe 2026! 🎉
