📝 About This Game

Phantom Circuit is a psychological horror puzzle game that blurs the line between the digital and the paranormal. Players navigate through a crumbling virtual cityscape, deciphering corrupted code and solving environmental puzzles while confronting the ghost of an AI gone rogue. Featuring a rich adaptive soundtrack and hand-drawn cel-shaded art direction, the game targets M-rated audiences on PC and Nintendo Switch.
