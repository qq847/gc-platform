Privacy Policy

How Game Connection collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: April 3, 2026 · Effective immediately
1

Who We Are

Game Connection is a B2B networking platform for the global video game industry, connecting game developers, publishers, investors, and event organizers. Our platform is operated by Game Connection SAS.

By using our platform at qq847.github.io/gc-platform, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information generated through your use of our platform:

  • Account information: Name, email address, profile photo (from Google Sign-In)
  • Professional information: Company name, job title, country, industry role
  • Activity data: Events you attend, projects you submit, meetings you schedule
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform

We use Google OAuth for authentication. We only request your basic profile information (name, email, profile photo). We do not access your Google contacts, Gmail, or any other Google services.

3

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to provide and improve the Game Connection platform:

  • Create and manage your professional profile
  • Match you with relevant events, projects, and business opportunities
  • Enable networking features (meeting scheduling, deal flow)
  • Send event reminders and platform notifications
  • Improve our AI-powered matching algorithms
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes outside of Game Connection.

4

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL database with row-level security), hosted on AWS infrastructure in the EU region.

  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Database access is restricted by role-based permissions
  • Authentication tokens are managed by Supabase Auth
  • We conduct regular security reviews

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

5

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our platform:

  • Google OAuth — Authentication (Google Privacy Policy applies)
  • Supabase — Database and authentication infrastructure
  • GitHub Pages — Website hosting
  • Vercel — API hosting

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

6

Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request permanent deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Opt out of certain data processing activities

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email below.

7

Cookies

We use minimal, essential cookies only:

  • Authentication cookies: To keep you logged in during your session
  • Preference cookies: To remember your platform settings

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

8

Children's Privacy

Game Connection is a professional B2B platform intended for adults (18+) working in the video game industry. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18 years of age.

9

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify users via email or a prominent notice on our platform. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

Questions or Requests?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your data rights, contact us directly.
