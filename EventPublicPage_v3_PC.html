📖 About This Event

Indie Game Fest 2026 is Europe's premier gathering for independent game developers, publishers, and investors. Over three intensive days at the Berlin Exhibition Center, attendees enjoy curated one-on-one meetings, keynote sessions from industry icons, an awards ceremony celebrating the year's best indie titles, and a public showcase floor featuring 60+ new games.

Whether you're an indie studio looking for your first publisher deal, an investor scouting the next breakout title, or a press member covering Europe's creative gaming scene — Indie Game Fest 2026 is the place to be.
