Our Mission

Making meaningful connections in the game industry effortless.

For over two decades, Game Connection has been the meeting point for the global video game industry. From our flagship events in San Francisco and Paris, to our year-round digital platform, we exist to help game companies find the right partners, investors, and opportunities.

Our platform is built on a simple belief: the best deals happen when the right people meet at the right time. We use AI-powered matching to make sure every connection counts.
