Echoes of Jade Dynasty is a breathtaking open-world action RPG drawing from the rich tapestry of Chinese mythology and ancient imperial history. Set across an 8km² seamless world, players unravel a conspiracy spanning three dynasties while mastering a fluid combat system inspired by classical martial arts. The game features 30+ fully voiced characters, branching narrative paths with meaningful consequences, and a proprietary weather-and-time system that dynamically reshapes enemy behavior and world events. With UE5-powered visuals and a fully orchestrated score, Echoes of Jade Dynasty sets a new benchmark for Eastern-inspired storytelling in the global RPG market.

🏢 About the Developer

CloudPeak Games

Founded in 2019 in Shanghai, CloudPeak Games is an independent studio of 12 passionate developers specializing in narrative-driven RPGs inspired by East Asian history and mythology. With a strong focus on artistic direction and player experience, the studio has shipped two critically acclaimed titles and built a loyal global fanbase. Echoes of Jade Dynasty is their most ambitious project to date, representing three years of dedicated development.
