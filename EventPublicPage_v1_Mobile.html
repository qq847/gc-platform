About This Event

Game Connection Live 2026 is the premier B2B event for the global video game industry, bringing together publishers, developers, investors, and service providers across Helsinki and Tallinn for six unforgettable days.

Whether you're an indie studio looking for your first publisher deal or an established studio seeking cutting-edge middleware solutions, GCL 2026 delivers the meetings, insights, and connections that drive the industry forward. Our AI-powered matchmaking engine helps you find exactly the right partners from 180+ exhibiting companies.

Spanning two Nordic capitals, the event features 48 curated sessions, a showcase of 60+ unreleased titles, and hundreds of pre-scheduled one-on-one meetings — all in stunning waterfront venues.
