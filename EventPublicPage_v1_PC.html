About the Event

Indie Game Fest 2026 is Europe's most celebrated gathering for independent game creators, publishers, investors, and passionate players. Set against the vibrant backdrop of Berlin, this three-day festival brings together the brightest minds in indie game development for an unparalleled showcase of creativity, innovation, and vision.

From world premiere game reveals and hands-on demos to curated business matchmaking sessions and high-level industry panels, IGF 2026 offers every attendee an immersive experience that spans every dimension of the indie game ecosystem. Whether you're a solo developer, a studio founder, a publisher scouting the next hit, or an investor looking for tomorrow's breakout title — this is your event.

With 23 carefully selected games competing across 6 award categories, exclusive networking facilitated through our Let's Meet platform, and a speaker lineup featuring over 50 industry leaders, Indie Game Fest 2026 is more than a conference — it's a launchpad for the next generation of independent games.
