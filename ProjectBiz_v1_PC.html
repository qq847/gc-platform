Description

Phantom Circuit is an atmospheric puzzle-horror game set in a decaying underground research facility. Players take the role of an ex-engineer navigating AI-generated illusions, solving environmental puzzles while uncovering a conspiracy that blurs reality and simulation. Heavily inspired by liminal aesthetics and J-horror cinema, the game features a fully procedural audio system and hand-painted environment art.
